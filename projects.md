## 🎬 The Marvel Multiverse: A Data-Driven Analysis of Cinematic Success

**Project Type:** Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

**Duration:** 2026

**Dataset:** Four Marvel movie and series datasets

### Overview

An analysis exploring what factors contribute to Marvel's cinematic success by examining the relationship between:

- Production budgets
- Box office performance
- Cast popularity
- Audience ratings

### Tools Used

Python • Pandas • Matplotlib • Seaborn

## Visualizations

### Budget vs Revenue

![Budget vs Revenue](../assets/images/budget-revenue.png)

### Correlation Heatmap

![Correlation Heatmap](../assets/images/correlation.png)

### Key Findings

- Higher budgets were associated with stronger box office performance.
- The MCU generated higher revenue compared to other Marvel universes.
- Cast popularity had little influence on audience ratings.
- Financial success does not always translate into better reviews.
